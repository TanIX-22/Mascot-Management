Mascot Management offers Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) services to help clients seeking to outsource some or all of their recruitment activities. We manage the end-to-end recruitment process — from profiling to onboarding of new employees — including staff, technology, reporting, and methods.

Our RPO services reduce time-to-hire and associated costs, offer measurable metrics, improve candidate pool quality, and ensure full compliance with government norms. We take complete ownership of the design and management of recruitment processes, ensuring smooth and effective hiring for our clients.

Mascot Management also provides Staffing Services to address manpower challenges during growth, project launches, business expansion, acquisitions, or slowdowns. We offer a stable platform that supports your workforce through every business phase — partnering in your strategy and freeing you from HR management complexities.

We leverage technology efficiently, delivering cost advantages and minimizing legal risks for our clients. Our services build trust, extract value, and ensure complete return on your investment.

Mascot Management specializes in sourcing professionals for flexible and on-demand business needs. We maintain a large and updated database of skilled profiles and follow a standard screening process that can be enhanced in collaboration with partners.

We offer manpower on-demand — including temporary, project-based, part-time, and consultancy roles — allowing clients to use these resources only when required. This flexible employment model is increasingly accepted by the professional community. We’ve consistently delivered quality talent across business segments, even on short notice.
